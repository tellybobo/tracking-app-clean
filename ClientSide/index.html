What we do

As a logistics management company, GLOBAL SHIPPING MAILS helps arrange port-to-port, door-to-door and terminal-to-terminal delivery for a wide range of goods. The company accepts both air and sea freight, and arranges to have goods transported to global destinations. Our logistics services include load and routing management, local storage and load consolidation, break bulk cargo packing, crating and loading services.
